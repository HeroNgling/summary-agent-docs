title: "Audio Playback"
description: "Re-listen to any part of your meeting with the built-in audio player."
---

Every recorded meeting includes full audio playback, available directly in the Fano Summary dashboard.

## Using the player

The audio player appears as a bar at the bottom of the meeting detail page. It shows:

- **Play/Pause button** — start or stop playback
- **Progress bar** — scrub to any point in the recording
- **Timestamp** — current position and total duration
- **Volume control** — adjust playback volume

## Navigating to specific moments

There are two ways to jump to a specific part of the meeting:

1. **Drag the progress bar** to scrub through the recording.
2. **Click a transcript segment** — the player jumps to the corresponding timestamp.

This makes it easy to re-listen to a specific discussion point without scrubbing through the entire recording.

<Tip>
Use the transcript as a table of contents for the audio. Find the topic you need in the transcript, click it, and listen to that section in context.
</Tip>

## Availability

Audio playback is available for all meetings — whether recorded via auto-join bot, Record Now, or file upload.
