## Step 1 — Accept your invitation

Your company admin will send you an invitation email with the subject line:

> "You've been invited to join [Company Name] on Fano Summary Agent"

Click **"Set Up Your Account"** in the email. You'll land on a setup page where you:

1. See your email (pre-filled) and company name.
2. Enter your **full name**.
3. Create a **password** (8+ characters, with uppercase, lowercase, and a number).
4. Click **"Activate Account."**

You're now logged in and taken to the Fano Summary dashboard.

<Info>
Already have an account? Go to [meetings.fanocloud.com](https://meetings.fanocloud.com) and sign in with your email and password.
</Info>

## Step 2 — Connect your calendar

Head to **Settings → Integration** and connect your **Microsoft Outlook** calendar. This lets Fano see your upcoming meetings and join them automatically.

See [Connect Your Calendar](/getting-started/connect-calendar) for the full walkthrough.

## Step 3 — Set your preferences

Before your first meeting, take 30 seconds to configure two things in **Settings**:

| Setting | Where to find it | What to set |
|---|---|---|
| **Summary Language** | Settings → Profile & Preferences | Choose the language for your AI summaries |
| **Bot Auto-join** | Settings → Bot Preferences | "Join all meetings" is on by default — leave it, or turn it off if you prefer manual recording |

## Step 4 — Have a meeting

That's it — just join your next call as usual. Fano will:

1. **Join automatically** as a visible note-taker bot.
2. **Record and transcribe** the entire meeting.
3. **Generate a summary** when the call ends.
4. **Email you a recap** if you have email notifications turned on.

## Step 5 — Review your notes

Open [meetings.fanocloud.com](https://meetings.fanocloud.com/app/meetings). Your meetings appear in the left sidebar, sorted by date. Click any meeting to see:

- **Summary** — AI-generated notes organized by topic
- **Transcript** — Full transcript with speaker labels and timestamps
- **Audio** — Playback bar at the bottom to re-listen to any part

<Tip>
Don't like the default summary format? Use the **template dropdown** above the summary to switch between formats like 1-on-1, Team Standup, Project Kick-off, and more. The summary regenerates instantly.
</Tip>
