Fano Memos is an AI-powered meeting assistant built by [Fano Labs](https://fanocloud.com). It joins your meetings automatically, captures everything that's said, and delivers a structured summary the moment the call ends.

## What Fano Memos does

**Records your meetings.** Fano joins your video calls as a note-taker bot, visible to all participants. It captures the full audio so nothing gets missed.

**Transcribes with speaker labels.** Every meeting gets a timestamped transcript with speakers identified, so you can see exactly who said what.

**Summarizes intelligently.** AI generates a structured summary — decisions, action items, key discussion points — organized by the template that fits your meeting type.

**Sends you a recap.** After the meeting ends, you'll receive an email recap with the summary, so you can catch up even if you missed the call.

## How it works

1. **Connect your calendar** — Link your Microsoft Outlook calendar so Fano can see your schedule.
2. **Fano joins automatically** — When a meeting starts, the Fano bot joins the call and begins recording.
3. **Review your notes** — After the meeting, open [Fano Memos](https://meetings.fanocloud.com) to view your summary, transcript, and audio.

That's it. No manual setup before each meeting, no forgetting to hit record.

## Supported platforms

Fano Memos currently works with:

- **Video conferencing:** Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet
- **Calendar:** Microsoft Outlook
