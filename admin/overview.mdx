As a Company Admin, you manage your organization's Fano Summary tenant — inviting team members, monitoring usage, and ensuring your team gets the most out of AI meeting notes.

## What admins can do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Invite members** | Add team members to your organization's Fano Summary account |
| **Manage users** | View member status (pending, active) and manage access |
| **Monitor usage** | Track your organization's meeting minutes consumption |
| **Configure tenant settings** | Set organization-level defaults |

## Getting started as an admin

When your organization is set up on Fano Summary, you'll receive an invitation email:

> "You've been invited to manage [Company Name] on Fano Summary Agent"

Click **"Set Up Your Account"** to:

1. See your email (pre-filled) and company name.
2. Enter your **full name**.
3. Create a **password** (8+ characters, with uppercase, lowercase, and a number).
4. Click **"Activate Account."**

You'll land directly on the Admin Dashboard, where you can start inviting team members.

## Your first steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate your account">
    Complete the setup flow from the invitation email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Add members so they can start using Fano Summary. See [Inviting Members](/admin/inviting-members).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your own calendar">
    Link your Microsoft Outlook calendar in Settings → Integration so your own meetings are recorded too.
  </Step>
</Steps>
