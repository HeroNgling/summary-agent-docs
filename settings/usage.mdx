The Usage page shows how many meeting minutes you've used this billing cycle and how much capacity remains.

## Viewing your usage

1. Click your **profile avatar** → **Setting**.
2. Go to the **Usage** tab.

You'll see:

- **Plan tier** — your current plan (e.g., Business)
- **Minutes used** — how many meeting minutes have been recorded this cycle
- **Minutes available** — your total monthly allowance
- **Usage percentage** — a progress bar showing how much of your allocation has been consumed
- **Reset date** — when your minutes reset for the next billing cycle

## How minutes are counted

Meeting minutes are counted based on the total duration of recorded meetings — whether captured by the auto-join bot, Record Now, or file upload.

<Info>
Minutes are counted per meeting, not per participant. A 30-minute meeting with 5 people uses 30 minutes of your allocation.
</Info>

## What happens when you reach your limit

If you've used all your allocated minutes for the month, new meetings will not be recorded until your usage resets. You'll still be able to access all previously recorded meetings, summaries, and transcripts.

Contact your company admin if you need additional capacity or a plan upgrade.
