Integrations let Fano Memos sync with your existing tools so meetings are captured automatically.

## Calendar integration

Fano Memos connects to your calendar to see upcoming meetings and send the note-taker bot to join them.

### Microsoft Outlook

**Status:** Available

Connect your Microsoft Outlook calendar to enable automatic meeting detection and bot joining.

1. Go to **Settings → Integration**.
2. Click **Connect** next to Microsoft Outlook.
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account and grant calendar access.
4. You'll see a **"Connected"** badge once linked.

To disconnect, click the **three-dot menu (⋯)** next to the integration and select **Disconnect**.

For more details, see [Connect Your Calendar](/getting-started/connect-calendar).

<Info>
Fano reads your calendar to detect meeting times and join links. It does not create, modify, or delete calendar events.
</Info>

## Managing integrations

All connected integrations appear on the **Integration** settings page with their current status. You can connect or disconnect integrations at any time without affecting your existing meeting recordings or summaries.
